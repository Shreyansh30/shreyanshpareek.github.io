Job Portal app

This project aims at ending poverty in our country, by helping/liberalizing the low wage workers to find work easily. Apart from them being able to find jobs, this project is a step towards eradicating poverty and building a stable market. This is implemented by providing a one stop platform for them, where they could find work posted by the recruiter. This is achieved through our app “Rozgaar”.
